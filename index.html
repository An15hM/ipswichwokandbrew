Ipswich Wok and Brew is a unique dining spot in Ipswich (24 Selkirk Road, IP4 3HX) blending the bold flavors of Japanese, Korean, and Nepali cuisine with the cozy, welcoming vibe of a local pub. Focused on community, quality, and creativity, we offer fusion dishes, craft beer pairings, and a relaxed space for casual meals or special gatherings—all made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a passion for authentic, feel-good food.
