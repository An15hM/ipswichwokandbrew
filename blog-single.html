Restaurant & Pub since 2023
A Unique Dining Experience

At Ipswich Wok and Brew, we combine the bold, vibrant flavours of traditional Asian cuisine with the warmth and comfort of a local pub. Our dishes are inspired by the rich culinary diversity of Asia, paired with a selection of craft beers to complement every bite. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a night out with friends, or a special occasion, we offer a welcoming atmosphere for everyone to enjoy.

  • Fusion Asian cuisine paired with craft beers
  • Local, seasonal ingredients for every dish
  • A community-focused environment for all occasions
The Story of Ipswich Wok and Brew
Welcome to Ipswich Wok and Brew
A Fusion of Flavor and Community

At Ipswich Wok and Brew, we blend the rich flavors of traditional Asian dishes with the inviting atmosphere of your favorite local pub. Our chef takes inspiration from the diverse culinary traditions of Asia, crafting fusion dishes designed to delight the senses.

We see our guests as part of our community. Every meal is an opportunity to create an experience that brings people together. Whether you’re here for a casual meal or a special event, we are dedicated to making every aspect of your dining experience exceptional. Join us for food, drinks, and memories!
